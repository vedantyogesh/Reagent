## Products and Solutions

### Battery Energy Storage (BES)

Ions Energy's battery energy storage systems (BES) use lithium iron phosphate (LFP) chemistry, which offers a superior safety profile, longer cycle life (4000+ cycles), and stable performance in Indian climate conditions compared to older lead-acid or NMC battery technologies.

BES systems are available in capacities from 5 kWh (suitable for homes) to 500 kWh and above (for industrial and commercial applications). Systems can be configured as standalone backup power, grid-tied with export capability, or paired with solar for hybrid operation.

Key features of Ions Energy BES systems:
- LFP battery chemistry with 10-year performance warranty
- Battery Management System (BMS) with remote monitoring
- Scalable modular design — capacity can be expanded post-installation
- Compatible with all major solar inverter brands
- MNRE-approved components
- Installation by certified technicians

### Hybrid Systems

Hybrid systems combine rooftop solar with battery energy storage, providing both daytime solar generation and stored energy for night-time or grid-outage use. This is the recommended configuration for customers in areas with frequent power cuts or high daytime tariffs.

Hybrid system benefits:
- Energy independence — generate and store your own power
- Backup power during grid outages (unlike grid-only solar which shuts down on outage)
- Peak shaving — use stored solar energy during expensive peak tariff hours
- Optimised for Indian grid conditions with frequent voltage fluctuations
- Single integrated system with unified monitoring

## BES vs Diesel Generator (DG)

Battery energy storage (BES) is increasingly preferred over diesel generators (DG sets) for backup power in India. Key comparison:

| Parameter | BES (Ions Energy) | Diesel Generator |
|---|---|---|
| Running cost | Near zero (charged from solar or grid) | ₹15–25/kWh (diesel at current prices) |
| Noise | Silent | 80–100 dB |
| Emissions | Zero | CO2, NOx, particulates |
| Maintenance | Minimal — BMS monitors automatically | Regular oil changes, filters, service |
| Switchover time | Instantaneous (< 20ms) | 10–30 seconds |
| Lifecycle | 10–15 years | 5–8 years |
| Fuel risk | None | Dependent on diesel availability and price |
| Space | Compact wall/floor mounted | Requires separate generator room |

For most commercial and residential applications, BES provides a better total cost of ownership over a 10-year horizon, particularly when paired with solar to reduce charging costs.

Diesel generators remain relevant for very large loads (above 500 kW continuous) or remote sites without grid access, where BES economics are less favourable.

## FAQs

### For Homeowners

**How much roof space do I need for solar?**
A typical 1 kW solar system requires approximately 8–10 square metres of unshaded roof space. A 3 kW system needs 24–30 sqm. The roof should ideally face south (in India) with no shading from trees or structures between 9 AM and 4 PM.

**Will solar work during a power cut?**
Standard grid-tied solar systems shut down during a power cut for safety reasons. To have backup power during outages, you need a hybrid system with a battery. Ions Energy's hybrid systems provide seamless switchover in under 20 milliseconds — lights and appliances remain on during a grid outage.

**How long does installation take?**
A standard residential rooftop solar installation takes 2–3 days for the physical installation. Net metering registration with the DISCOM typically adds 2–6 weeks depending on the state and utility. Ions Energy handles all DISCOM paperwork on your behalf.

**What maintenance is required?**
Solar panels need cleaning every 1–2 months (more frequently in dusty areas). The inverter and BMS are self-monitoring and alert you via the app if any issue is detected. Ions Energy offers annual maintenance contracts (AMC) for peace of mind.

**What happens to excess solar energy?**
If your system is grid-connected with net metering, excess energy is exported to the grid and you receive credit on your electricity bill. If you have a battery, excess solar charges the battery first before export.

**Is there government subsidy for rooftop solar?**
Yes. PM Surya Ghar Muft Bijli Yojana provides subsidies for residential rooftop solar up to 3 kW. Ions Energy helps with subsidy applications as part of the installation process.

### For Businesses

**What is the payback period for commercial solar?**
For most commercial installations in India, the payback period is 4–6 years depending on the tariff rate, system size, and utilisation. With a 25-year panel warranty, this represents a strong ROI.

**Can we install solar on a leased premises?**
Yes, with landlord consent. Ions Energy can structure the agreement to protect both tenant and landlord interests. Alternatively, a RESCO (Renewable Energy Service Company) model may be available where Ions Energy owns the system and sells power at a fixed rate below grid tariff.

**What happens to the system if we relocate?**
Solar systems can be dismantled and reinstalled at a new location. There is a relocation cost (typically 15–20% of original installation cost). Battery systems are more easily relocated than rooftop solar.

**Do you handle DISCOM approvals for commercial connections?**
Yes. Ions Energy manages the full DISCOM interconnection and net metering application process for commercial customers. Our team is experienced with approvals across multiple states and DISCOMs.

**Can we finance the installation?**
Yes. Ions Energy has tie-ups with NBFCs and banks for solar financing at competitive rates. RESCO (zero capex, pay-per-unit) and operating lease models are also available for qualifying businesses.
